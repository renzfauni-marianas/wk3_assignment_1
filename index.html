Saipan's Must Try Restaurants

Food is something you must always experience when you travel, so in this article, I would like to give you my top picks on where to dine when you are visiting Saipan.

Best Places to Dine:

  1. Shirley's Cafe
  2. Thunder Chicken
  3. Kensington Resort
  4. West Coast Restaurant
  5. J's Restaurant

Shirley's Cafe | Susupe, Saipan

Shirley's Cafe is a restaurant that serves a variety of food. Their menu contains a number of delectible drinks and dishes that you are sure to be feasting on. They have plenty space to sit with entertainment.

What I Love:
  • Their Delicious Food
  • Very Affordable
  • Good Customer Service
